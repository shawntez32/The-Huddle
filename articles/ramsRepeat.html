5 Reasons the Rams will repeat as super bowl champs

Reason number 1 they're ahead of schedule. This was only season 1 for Sean Mcvay and Matthew Stafford. When the trade happened the sports world was stunned, 2 First Round Picks!? For a guy who never won a playoff game. Jared Goff had gotten them to the super bowl and lost, so for it to make any sense they would have to win the superbowl. Well they did just that. A year later that trade is looking like a steal and there have been many similar deals this past off-season as much of the league is adopting the Rams approach. The Rams are ahead of schedule like they always are.
