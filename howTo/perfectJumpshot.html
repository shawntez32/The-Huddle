How to shoot a jumpshot

In the new era of basketball we have become jumpshot happy. That's fine the game is always evolving, but with that playing style a certain skill becomes crucial. The art of the jumpshot...

Square your feet shoulder width apart, do a natural bend with knees and waist. Elbow parralel to shoulder on your shooting hand off hand to guide it eyes locked in on target natural jump aim and fire.
